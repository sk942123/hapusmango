How to Ripen Your Mangoes at Home

    
Devgad Hapus is sent slightly raw to protect it during transit. Follow these steps for perfect ripeness.

  

  

    
1
Keep at Room Temp
Place mangoes in a cool, dry spot — not in the fridge, not in direct sunlight. Keep them in the box with the straw for the first 2–3 days.

    
2
Check on Day 3–4
Look for a golden-yellow colour appearing from the top near the stem. A light floral aroma will start to fill the room — that's the sign.

    
3
Press Gently to Test
A ripe Devgad Hapus gives slightly to gentle pressure — firm but not hard. If still very firm, give it another day.

    
4
Enjoy!
Once ripe, refrigerate and consume within 2–3 days. Never refrigerate unripe mangoes — it stops the ripening process permanently.

What's the Difference?

  

  

    
Feature🥭 Devgad Alphonso (Ours)🥭 Ratnagiri Alphonso

    
      
AromaIntense, saffron-floral, fills the roomFragrant but slightly milder

Pulp colourDeep saffron-orangeGolden yellow-orange

      
TextureSilky smooth, zero fibre, melts on tongueSmooth, occasionally slightly fibrous
